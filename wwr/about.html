Our History

Founded in 1990, our company has offered unique rafting experiences in the best places throughout the country. It is an activity which we take to its maximum expression where family, friends and adventurers get together to experience new emotions and where water is a part of it. Since its founding we have always sought to make this activity something out of the ordinary, and was born in the experience that the founders had with their families to share something that generated curiosity and excitement.
